OUR VISION

Our vision is to create a greener environment in the deforest prone communities in Sierra Leone and to restore loss of vegetation within Sierra Leone and to create afforestation for biodiversity and for research purposes, and to feed the growing population (SDG-2) to achieve sustainable development using our all – inclusive concept.

Read More

WHAT WE DO

We create campaigns, projects, and programs, in order to promote youth and women empowerment, climate change mitigation and adaption, regenerative agriculture to feed the growing population, and the successful implementation of SDGs by 2030 for a better world.

Read More

OUR MISSION

We promote the awareness raising education campaign about climate crisis, Environmental protection, Conversation of forestry, Afforestation of different improved varieties of Economic trees globally (SGD-13) Climate Action and to Integrate solution for biodiversity conservation, ecosystem preservation, disaster preparedness and prevention at Country level.

Read More

AFFORESTATION OF ECONOMIC TREES

We embark on baboon trees, timber, oil palms planting. Economic trees, orange, lemon, cashew nuts, mango, pear etc. all to mitigate the level of climate change and its surrounding crisis as they will immensely contribute to reduce the negative impact of green house gas emissions and global warming.
